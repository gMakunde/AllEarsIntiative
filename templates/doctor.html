Things to ask your doctor.

A suggested way to talk to your doctor about these findings.

"I was reading online about my symptoms and I came across these possible complications. What tests can be performed in order for me to better understand what is going on. Also, what are some preventative measures that I take in order to decrese my chances of getting complication or making it worse." If your doctor refuses to give you the tests, ask them to record it in their records. In the case something happens the doctor can be held accountable for neglegence.

Based on our system, your symptoms suggest that you are at risk for: Preclampsia.

Remeber this is not a diagnosis, so please make sure to talk to a doctor before taking any further steps.

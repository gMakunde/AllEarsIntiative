The Motherboard

The MotherBoard gives you the unique opportunity to document any pregnancy symptoms that you may be experiencing. Based on these symptoms, you will see possible complications that you could be experiencing or are susceptible to and a guide for each complication. These guides will explain the given complication in detail and possible treatment options that can be discussed with their regular physician. The MotherBoard will also have features such as Baby Stats, Things to Ask Your Doctor, Tip of the Day, and a Community page.

Mission

The mission of the All Ears Initiative is to empower and educate expecting and new mothers. We are striving to increase awareness of maternal health while simultaneously decreasing terribly high maternal mortality rates in women of color. These alarming rates are fueled by racial disparities and miseducation. We will make an impact by creating a safe space for mothers of color to not only learn, but also to have community amongst themselves. Our goal is not to replace medical doctors, but to give mothers the resources they need to better understand their situation to ensure more productive visits to the doctor.

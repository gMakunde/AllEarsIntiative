How are you feeling?

This section allows you to document symptoms you may be experiencing for a better understanding of what complications are linked to them. Documenting daily is encouraged if you experience symptoms more than usual. Using a machine learning algorithm, the motherboard evaluates your daily symptoms for possible linkage to pregnancy complications.

Things to ask your doctor.

Once you have documented your symptoms, this section will give you an analysis of possible complications that are linked to your symptoms. Not only do you get the analysis, but each complication listed will also come with a guide to explain what the complication is in depth and possible treatment options. The guide will help you better communicate with your doctor regarding your concerns!
